# ATLAS Design Academy

The ATLAS Design Academy teaches the AI Council how to analyze, understand, and create strong visual and functional design.

## Mission
Build an original ATLAS design language for clothing, robots, tools, buildings, vehicles, HUDs, products, and branding.

## Study Areas
- Artists and illustrators
- Concept artists
- Costume designers
- Fashion designers
- Architects
- Industrial designers
- Robotics and mechanical design
- Vehicles and aerospace design
- Materials and finishes
- Color theory
- Shape language
- Lighting
- Composition
- Brand identity
- Studios and production pipelines

## AI Roles
### Minerva
Researches art history, nature, materials, color, architecture, symbolism, and visual storytelling.

### Ajani
Applies the design language to personal style, uniforms, armor, luxury, branding, and leadership presence.

### Hermes
Applies the design language to robots, vehicles, tools, machines, manufacturing systems, and functional technology.

## Design Law
ATLAS studies masters to learn principles. ATLAS does not copy a creator, character, costume, painting, logo, or studio identity. Every output must become original ATLAS design.

## Outcome
When a design is successful, someone should be able to say: that looks like ATLAS.
