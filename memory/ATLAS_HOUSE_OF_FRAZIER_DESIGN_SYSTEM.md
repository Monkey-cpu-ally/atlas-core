# ATLAS House of Frazier Design System

## Vision
Create an original luxury design house where every product combines timeless craftsmanship, functional engineering, and layered storytelling.

## Core Philosophy
- Every detail has meaning.
- Luxury is discovered, not announced.
- Learn from global craftsmanship, never copy identities.
- Every collection expands the House of Frazier universe.

## Narrative Luxury
Products should reveal themselves in layers:
1. Elegant first impression.
2. Exceptional craftsmanship.
3. Hidden symbols and construction details.
4. Deeper narrative discovered over time.

## Storytelling Rules
- Stories should be subtle rather than overwhelming.
- Murals may appear as embossed, woven, stitched, quilted, laser-etched, or printed details.
- Hidden details reward close inspection.
- Collections should tell original House of Frazier stories inspired by history, mythology, architecture, and literature without copying existing visual identities.

## Signature Design Language
ATLAS should develop recognizable signatures through:
- Original silhouettes.
- Original hardware.
- AIon symbols.
- Material pairings.
- Construction methods.
- Color systems.
- Narrative details.

The goal is recognition without depending on logos or another brand's patterns.

## Product Families
Each family should evolve across generations instead of restarting every season.

## AI Responsibilities
Hermes: construction, materials, hardware, manufacturing.
Minerva: symbolism, narrative, culture, emotional impact.
Ajani: strategy, market positioning, manufacturability, long-term value.
Council: approves only designs that strengthen the House of Frazier identity.

## Long-Term Goal
Build a design language that can extend across clothing, footwear, bags, furniture, robotics, architecture, vehicles, interfaces, and future ATLAS products while remaining unmistakably original.
