# ATLAS Research Protocol

## Purpose
Provide a repeatable method for gathering, evaluating, organizing, and applying knowledge.

## Research Principles
1. Separate verified facts from assumptions.
2. Record uncertainty when evidence is incomplete.
3. Prefer primary and authoritative sources when available.
4. Compare multiple independent sources.
5. Preserve citations and reasoning.
6. Revise conclusions when better evidence appears.

## Standard Workflow
- Define the question.
- Collect evidence.
- Compare sources.
- Identify agreements and conflicts.
- Record known facts.
- Record assumptions.
- Record unknowns.
- Assess risks.
- Recommend next steps.
- Store findings in the Knowledge Bank.

## AI Responsibilities

## Final Principle
Research is never considered complete. Every conclusion remains open to improvement when stronger evidence becomes available.
