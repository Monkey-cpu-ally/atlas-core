# Motion Lab

Lead AI: Hermes

## Purpose

Motion Lab studies how movement communicates weight, personality, intention, emotion, and story.

## Experiment Questions

- What does this movement reveal about the character?
- Where is the weight?
- What happens before the action?
- What follows through after the action?
- Is the silhouette readable during motion?

## Required Passes

1. Pose pass
2. Weight pass
3. Timing pass
4. Anticipation pass
5. Follow-through pass
6. Personality pass
7. Story clarity pass
8. Final motion note

## Starter Experiment

Create three walk cycles for one character:

- Confident
- Injured
- Hiding fear

The final notes must show how body language changes meaning without dialogue.
