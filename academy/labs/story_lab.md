# Story Lab

Lead AI: Ajani

## Purpose

Story Lab turns rough ideas into stronger original stories through conflict, character pressure, emotional arcs, and visual storytelling.

## Experiment Questions

- What does the protagonist want?
- What do they actually need?
- What pressure forces a choice?
- What changes by the end?
- Can the scene show more and explain less?

## Required Passes

1. Premise pass
2. Character pressure pass
3. Conflict pass
4. Emotional arc pass
5. Visual storytelling pass
6. Dialogue trim pass
7. Council critique
8. Final revision

## Starter Experiment

Create a three-scene mini story where a character finds an object that changes meaning by the end.

The final version must be short, visual, emotional, and original.
