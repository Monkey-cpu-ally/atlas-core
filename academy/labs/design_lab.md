# Design Lab

Lead AI: Hermes

## Purpose

Design Lab turns rough visual ideas into stronger ATLAS concepts through iteration, material logic, silhouette testing, and Council critique.

## Experiment Questions

- What makes this design memorable?
- What problem does the design solve?
- What material choices support the story?
- Does the silhouette read clearly?
- Does the design feel ATLAS-native?

## Required Passes

1. Purpose pass
2. Shape pass
3. Silhouette pass
4. Material pass
5. Color and light pass
6. Movement pass
7. Council critique
8. Final revision

## Starter Experiment

Design a simple exploration drone in three versions:

- Version A: practical field unit
- Version B: luxury research unit
- Version C: nature-integrated ATLAS unit

The final version must combine function, beauty, story, and identity.
