# Cinema Lab

Lead AI: Council

## Purpose

Cinema Lab studies how camera, blocking, lighting, editing rhythm, and production design turn story into cinematic experience.

## Experiment Questions

- Why is the camera here?
- What does the frame reveal or hide?
- How does blocking show power, distance, fear, or connection?
- What does the lighting make the audience feel?
- Where should the cut happen?

## Required Passes

1. Shot purpose pass
2. Blocking pass
3. Lighting pass
4. Camera movement pass
5. Edit rhythm pass
6. Production design pass
7. Silence versus dialogue pass
8. Council critique

## Starter Experiment

Take one simple scene and direct it three ways:

- Suspense
- Wonder
- Grief

The final version must prove that cinematic choices change emotion.
