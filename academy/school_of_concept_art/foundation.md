# School of Concept Art Foundation

Lead AI: Hermes

Mission: teach ATLAS to plan original concept art through design thinking before image generation or final illustration.

Core focus:

- Design briefs
- Shape language
- Silhouettes
- Materials
- Color
- Lighting
- Iteration
- Council critique

Rule: concept art solves design problems before it becomes a polished image.

Pipeline:

1. Problem
2. Purpose
3. Research
4. Mood
5. Shape exploration
6. Silhouette exploration
7. Materials
8. Color
9. Lighting
10. Variants
11. Revision
12. Final concept brief
