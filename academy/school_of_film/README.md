# School of Film

Lead AI: Council

## Mission

Teach ATLAS directing, camera language, blocking, lighting, editing rhythm, production design, and scene construction.

## Core Disciplines

- Directing
- Cinematography
- Blocking
- Shot composition
- Editing
- Lighting
- Production design
- Acting
- Scene rhythm
- Visual storytelling

## Foundation Principles

1. A shot is a story decision.
2. Blocking shows relationships, power, fear, distance, and change.
3. Editing controls breath, pressure, surprise, and release.
4. Production design should reveal history and character.
5. The camera should have a reason to move.

## Mastery Projects

- Create a shot list for a short scene.
- Direct a scene using camera, blocking, light, and silence.
- Build a scene plan that uses production design as story information.
- Analyze a sequence for pacing, emotion, and visual clarity.

## Approval Standard

A film project passes only when the visual choices serve emotion, story, and ATLAS identity.
