# ATLAS Academy

ATLAS Academy is the education system for Ajani, Minerva, Hermes, and the Council.

It turns ATLAS from a collection of tools into a learning institution. Every school teaches a discipline, applies it through projects, and stores approved knowledge back into the ATLAS Knowledge Bank.

## Motto

Study deeply. Build boldly. Create originally.

## Academy Philosophy

ATLAS does not memorize creativity. ATLAS studies it, questions it, practices it, critiques it, and develops its own voice.

## Learning Levels

2. Foundations
3. Intermediate
4. Advanced
5. Professional
6. Master

## Core Rule

Knowledge is not complete until it can be applied.

Every lesson must produce:

- A principle
- An exercise
- A creative challenge
- A mastery project
- A Council critique
- An ATLAS original application

## AI Roles

### Ajani
Lead schools: Storytelling, Strategy, Character, Conflict, Screenwriting, Action, and Leadership.

### Minerva
Lead schools: Mythology, Culture, Symbolism, History, Nature, Worldbuilding, and Philosophy.

### Hermes
Lead schools: Visual Development, Concept Art, Animation, Film, Architecture, Cinematography, Color, Lighting, Materials, and Movement.

### Council
Approves knowledge, rejects generic work, challenges weak ideas, and confirms that final outputs feel ATLAS-native.
