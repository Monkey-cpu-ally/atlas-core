# ATLAS Blueprint Bank

## Purpose
The Blueprint Bank stores build plans, diagrams, CAD references, part breakdowns, material lists, wiring notes, systems diagrams, assembly logic, and prototype documentation.

## AI Owner
Ajani — Chief Engineer.

## Supporting AIs
- Hermes — Chief Robotics & Design: CAD, robotics, product form, interface design.
- Minerva — Chief Scientist: biomaterials, biological systems, environmental constraints.
- Council — Executive Board: approval, risk review, and project prioritization.

## Allowed Content
- Mechanical blueprints
- Electrical diagrams
- CAD and 3D model notes
- PCB references
- Materials and tools lists
- Assembly/disassembly procedures
- Risk and safety notes
- Prototype records

## Future Automation Notes
ATLAS should connect blueprints to projects, simulations, digital twins, materials, costs, and tests. Every major invention should have a blueprint record before physical prototyping.
