# ATLAS Knowledge Bank

## Purpose
The Knowledge Bank stores structured knowledge learned from approved sources, research pipelines, documents, courses, books, papers, videos, patents, and personal notes.

## Allowed Content
- Summaries and key findings
- Source links and citations
- Confidence levels
- Verified facts and open questions
- Topic maps and relationships
- Research notes and extracted concepts
- World Knowledge Network source records
