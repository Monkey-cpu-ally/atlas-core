# ATLAS Vault

## Purpose
The Vault stores sensitive, private, strategic, legal, invention, investor, family, and confidential planning material.

## AI Owner
Council — Executive Board.

## Supporting AIs
- Ajani — Chief Engineer: invention feasibility, patent engineering notes, technical risk.
- Hermes — Chief Robotics & Design: confidential designs, prototype visuals, CAD strategy.
- Minerva — Chief Scientist: sensitive science, biology, medical, plant, and environmental research notes.

## Allowed Content
- Invention notes
- Patent drafts
- Confidential project strategy
- Investor material
- Legal planning notes
- Family-only material
- Permission-controlled records

## Future Automation Notes
The Vault should require stronger permissions than normal banks. ATLAS must not ingest, expose, or summarize Vault material without explicit user permission. Future versions should support encryption, access roles, audit trails, and approval gates.
