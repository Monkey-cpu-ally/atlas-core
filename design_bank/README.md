# ATLAS Design Bank

## Purpose
The Design Bank stores ATLAS visual references, design principles, style systems, and aesthetic knowledge. It is separate from the Memory Bank and Knowledge Bank so design inspiration does not get mixed with operational memory or factual research.

## Primary AI Owner
Hermes — Chief Robotics & Design.

## Supporting AIs
- Ajani — Chief Engineer: validates whether design choices can be engineered, manufactured, powered, and maintained.
- Minerva — Chief Scientist: validates natural, biological, environmental, and material inspiration.
- Council — Executive Board: resolves tradeoffs and approves major design direction.

## Allowed Content
- Artists and visual style references
- Architecture references
- Clothing and fashion systems
- Robotics design language
- Industrial design references
- Vehicles, aircraft, and ships
- Color theory
- Typography and UI/UX notes
- Logos and brand identity
- Figma-linked design notes

## Automation Notes
Future ATLAS automation should allow Hermes to index approved Figma files, uploaded images, sketches, screenshots, and design references. ATLAS should store summaries, tags, relationships, and source links rather than blindly copying copyrighted materials.
