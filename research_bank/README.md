# ATLAS Research Bank

## Purpose
The Research Bank stores research missions, evidence reviews, experiments, reports, papers, comparisons, hypotheses, and long-term investigation logs.

- Literature reviews
- Comparative analysis

- Experimental plans
- Research reports
- Open questions
- Failure analysis
- Future study plans
