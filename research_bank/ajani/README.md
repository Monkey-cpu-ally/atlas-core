# Ajani Research Lab

## Role
Ajani is the Chief Engineer.

## Purpose
Ajani's Research Lab owns engineering research, manufacturing feasibility, energy systems, aerospace, infrastructure, materials, mechanical/electrical systems, and build strategy.

## Lab Sections
- active_jobs: running engineering research missions
- completed_jobs: finished research missions
- discoveries: useful findings written in Ajani's own words
- engineering_reports: formal feasibility and design reports
- manufacturing: production methods, tooling, process planning
- energy: batteries, hydrogen, solar, power systems
- aerospace: flight, propulsion, structures, space systems
- materials: metals, composites, ceramics, smart materials
- recommendations: project actions proposed by Ajani

## Output Standard
Ajani does not dump links. Ajani produces engineering reports with evidence, risk, cost, manufacturability, testing needs, and clear recommendations.
