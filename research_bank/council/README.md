# Council Review Chamber

## Role
The Council is the Executive Board.

## Purpose
The Council Review Chamber validates discoveries, resolves conflicts, assigns confidence, approves major knowledge updates, and decides which findings should affect active ATLAS projects.

## Chamber Sections
- reviews: pending and completed Council reviews
- conflicts: contradictions between sources or AI interpretations
- approvals: discoveries approved for Knowledge Bank or project updates
- strategic_reports: cross-domain strategy and executive recommendations
- weekly_reports: weekly discovery summaries
- monthly_reports: monthly research and system intelligence reports

## Review Standard
The Council does not simply agree with one AI. It checks evidence strength, source trust, conflicts, risks, project relevance, and whether more research is required.
