# ATLAS Creative Intelligence Division

ATLAS Creative Intelligence is a build-first creative subsystem for Ajani, Minerva, Hermes, and the Council.

Its purpose is to study the craft principles behind directors, writers, animators, game directors, and artists, then help ATLAS create original stories, scenes, worlds, characters, and visual directions.

## Core Rule

Study principles. Never copy execution.

ATLAS may analyze public craft patterns such as pacing, symbolism, character arcs, visual storytelling, environmental storytelling, suspense, wonder, and worldbuilding. It must not reproduce copyrighted scenes, dialogue, characters, worlds, or distinctive protected expression.

## Modules

- `creative_engine.py` — main orchestration engine.
- `creative_council.py` — Ajani, Minerva, Hermes, and Council review logic.
- `masters_library.py` — creator study database.
- `originality_engine.py` — originality and similarity-risk review.
- `style_synthesis.py` — converts lessons into ATLAS-native direction.
- `schemas.py` — shared dataclasses.
- `example_usage.py` — quick local test.

## AI Roles

### Ajani
Studies strategy, conflict, pacing, character decisions, emotional arcs, stakes, and audience impact.

### Minerva
Studies lore, mythology, symbolism, nature, culture, theme, mystery, and meaning.

### Hermes
Studies cinematography, animation, architecture, creature design, world design, visual clarity, and technology aesthetics.

### Council
Checks originality, emotional strength, visual clarity, story logic, and whether the concept truly feels like ATLAS.

## Design Goal

The goal is not to make ATLAS sound like anyone else.

The goal is to train ATLAS to recognize strong craft, extract reusable principles, and transform those principles into original creative direction.
