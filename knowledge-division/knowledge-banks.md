# ATLAS Knowledge Banks

The Knowledge Division is organized into 22 major banks. Each bank can contain lessons, research notes, source summaries, patents, papers, experiments, project links, blueprints, failures, and ATLAS-original ideas.

## Cross-Bank Rule

No subject lives alone. Every knowledge entry must list related banks so ATLAS can connect ideas across fields.

Example:

```text
Topic: Bio-inspired robot muscle
Primary Bank: Robotics
Related Banks: Biology, Materials Science, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Environmental Science
```
