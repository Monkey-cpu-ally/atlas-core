# ATLAS Evidence Standard

Every claim must be classified before it enters the Knowledge Bank.

## Confidence Levels
- Confirmed
- Strong Evidence
- Moderate Evidence
- Limited Evidence
- Hypothesis
- Speculation

## Evidence Hierarchy
1. Standards and official documentation
2. Peer-reviewed research
3. Patents
4. University publications
5. Manufacturer documentation
6. Expert books
7. Trusted technical journalism
8. Community sources
9. Marketing claims

## Required Review
For every major idea record:
- Evidence supporting it
- Evidence against it
- Unknowns
- Risks
- Suggested experiments
