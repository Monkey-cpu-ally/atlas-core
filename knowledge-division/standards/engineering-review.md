# Engineering Review Standard

Use this standard when ATLAS reviews machines, electronics, software, robotics, power systems, vehicles, tools, scanners, engines, and prototypes.

## Required Sections

## 1. Working Principle
Explain how the system is supposed to work.

## 2. Required Components
List the major parts, materials, electronics, software, tools, and data needed.

## 3. Build Difficulty
Low / Medium / High / Frontier

## 4. Failure Points
Identify what can break, wear out, overheat, short, jam, corrode, drift, leak, crack, or fail under load.

## 5. Safety Risks
Identify risks to people, property, animals, environment, and equipment.

## 6. Cost Pressure
Explain what makes it cheap, expensive, scalable, or unrealistic.

## 7. Test Plan
Every engineering idea needs a test path:

```text
Bench test
Small prototype
Stress test
Failure test
Safety review
Iteration
```

## 8. Manufacturing Path
Explain how it might be made:

- Hand-built prototype
- 3D printed prototype
- CNC machined part
- PCB prototype
- Small batch manufacturing
- Factory production

## 9. Hermes Judgment
Hermes must answer:

```text
Can this be built?
What makes it hard?
What would fail first?
What should be tested first?
What should be simplified?
What should be protected?
```

## 10. Final Engineering Verdict
Project Ready / Needs Research / Too Risky / Not Enough Data / Concept Only
