| SRC-ACA-0004 | Research Institutes | Public/private institutes | Research publications | All Banks | Tier A/B | Catalog Scaffold | Review each institute and publication separately. |

## Rules

- Academic sources still need source passports.
- Peer review increases trust, but does not remove the need to check date, limitations, and conflicts.
- Old academic sources may be historically important but scientifically outdated.
