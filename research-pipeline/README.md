# ATLAS Research Pipeline

The Research Pipeline is the controlled knowledge-gathering system for ATLAS.

It helps ATLAS collect sources, judge reliability, extract claims, connect findings to projects, and route knowledge to Ajani, Minerva, Hermes, or the Council.

---

# Core Files

- `ARCHITECTURE.md` — defines the full research workflow.
- `SOURCE_NOTE_TEMPLATE.md` — template for recording sources and evidence.

---

# Pipeline Stages

1. Source intake
2. Source classification
3. Reliability rating
4. Claim extraction
5. Project mapping
6. AI routing
7. Graph Memory update
8. Research report

---

# Evidence Rule

ATLAS can take inspiration from anywhere, but proof must come from strong sources.

Research notes must separate:

- Proven facts
- Strong evidence
- Promising evidence
- Early research
- Plausible assumptions
- Speculation
- Unsupported claims
- Unsafe or rejected claims
